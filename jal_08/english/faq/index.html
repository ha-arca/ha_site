Q&A

faq

Here are answers to the questions most frequently asked by our guests.

Where is aboard buses in Haneda airport?

We can pick you up from international terminal, terminal 1, terminal 2.
Please view the time and place in detail at [shuttle bus]

Where is the nearest station?

Around 3 minutes walking distance to Anamoriinari-Station of Keihinkyukou-line. Around 10 minutes walking distance to Tenkuubashi-Station of Tokyo monorail.
※We put timetable at the front desk.

Is there a convenience store nearby?

Front of hotel: Circle K Sunkus
Walking about 3 minutes to Lawson
Walking about 3 minutes to Family mark
Walking about 10 minutes to Seven-Eleven
※We put map at the front desk.
※View map here

Can you use internet in the room?

All rooms are equipped high-speed LAN line. We are using optical line in hotel, and VDSL into guest rooms.
※LAN cable is available in all guest rooms. You can use wifi in hotel.

How much do you charge for a late checkout?

Extra fee is as below.
30% of accommodation cost if 3 hours late.
50% of accommodation cost if 6 hours late.
100% of accommodation cost if over 6 hours late.

About cancellation charges

Base on accommodation cost, cancellation charges are as below:
The days before your arrival date is 20%
On your arrival date is 80%
No contact is 100%
※Cancellation charges may be required if group cancel due to the difference rules.

About check-in and check-out

Check-in any time after 14:00
Check-out by 11:00
(※Each package has a different time schedule or late check-out free. Please contact us.)

About non-smoking room

Please request non-smoking room when booking.
※Depending on the room vacancy of the day, we may not be able to meet your request. Thank you for your understanding in advance.

About parking area

Hotel’s coin parking area capacity is 9 vehicles.
Please contact us before using it. If you do not book in advance, you can not use.
※If parking area was full, please use nearby parking area. Hotel will not pay for this fee.
※On holidays such as GW, Christmas, End-New Year days, Sunday, parking area is crowded. we recommend using public transport.
※You can refer coin parking times at here but please book space with hotel by phone.
Tel: 03-5735-2525

Sending luggage in advance via home-delivery service

Luggage sent to hotel by Delivery Service will be kept at hotel Front Desk.
Please write the reservation name, name when sending luggage.
We will give back to you at check-in time.
※You also can use delivery service to send luggage from hotel by Yamato transport service.
If you want the other services, please contact us.

Using room for over 3 persons

We provide rooms for 2 persons (double room or twin room).
There is no extra bed or sofa bed.
*If you have small children or want any extra bed, please contact us.

Left luggage in the room.

Any left luggage will be kept within a specific period of time. Please contact us to claim it back. After that period, we take it to the Police Service.

About bank transfer payment

Please contact us for payment in advance.
Please effect transfer 5 working days (excluding Saturday and Sunday) BEFORE arrive to hotel
If you do not meet deadline, please FAX the transfer confirmation to hotel.

About payment

Please pay upon check-in as there are early checkout customers. Thanks for your understanding.

Are there restaurants nearby the hotel?

Across Kanjou Route 8 front of the hotel, there are Guydon shop and Ramen shop open until to 2:00AM. Hotel front desk can recommend you seafood restaurants in Haneda that open from afternoon to 21:00PM. Should there be any queries about food shop, you can always contact us.
